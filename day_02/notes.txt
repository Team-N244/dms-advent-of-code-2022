These are the possible choices:
A = Opponent Playing Rock
B = Opponent Playing Paper
C = Opponent Playing Scissors

X = You Play Rock     (1pt)
Y = You Play Paper    (2pts)
Z = You Play Scissors (3pts)

A X -> 1pt + 3pt (draw)
A Y -> 2pt + 6pt (win)
A Z -> 3pt + 0pt (loss)

B X -> 1pt + 0pt (loss)
B Y -> 2pt + 3pt (draw)
B Z -> 3pt + 6pt (win)

C X -> 1pt + 6pt (win)
C Y -> 2pt + 0pt (lose)
C Z -> 3pt + 3pt (draw)
